# Document Register | YOTEL + YOTELPAD Barbados

**Source Tier Hierarchy:**
- **T1:** Statute, regulation, official planning instrument, official approval form, official boundary definition
- **T2:** Survey plans, parcel geometry, legal descriptions, land records, georeferenced plans
- **T3:** Negotiated brand standards, hotel technical requirements, design briefs
- **T4:** Project investment memo, capex model, internal concept deck, development presentation
- **T5:** Inferred assumptions or placeholders

**Conflict Rule:** If two sources conflict, both are stored. The conflict is flagged. The higher-tier source is active unless explicitly overridden by a documented decision.
