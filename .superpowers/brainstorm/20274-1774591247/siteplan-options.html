Mt Brevitor Estates — Site Plan Options

120 acres, elevated hillside, St Peter, Barbados — which spatial logic?

Based on research: trade winds blow NE→SW, west coast views face left, gully runs through eastern edge, 6-acre agricultural parcel is designated. Click to select your preferred approach.

A: Hub-and-Spoke

Community hub at centre — all clusters radiate outward. Density decreases with distance from hub. Premium hilltop has best views. Farm downwind (SE). X Range separated NW with noise buffer. Gully buffer on east edge.

Pros

  • All residents within 10-min walk of hub
  • Natural density gradient (entry near hub, premium far)
  • Farm odour/noise downwind from all residential
  • Premium cluster gets hilltop views

Cons

  • X Range isolated from foot traffic
  • Longer infrastructure runs to perimeter
  • Single hub creates congestion at peak hours

B: Linear Spine

Single spine road from gate to hilltop — clusters branch off sequentially. Entry at bottom, premium at top. X Range integrated with community hub as anchor destination. Farm buffers gully on east side.

Pros

  • X Range + Community Hub = combined anchor destination
  • Simpler infrastructure (single spine)
  • Natural "journey" from entry → premium up the hill
  • Farm + gully create green eastern edge

Cons

  • Premium cluster furthest from amenities
  • Single-road bottleneck risk
  • X Range noise closer to mid-range residential

Research-Based Placement Logic

Farm (SE/E): Dairy odour/noise downwind (NE→SW trade winds). 50m+ buffer from residential. Gully adjacency for water runoff.
X Range (NW/W): 300-400m range length oriented away from residential. F&B as community draw. Min 1,000ft safety zone.
Solar (S-facing): Barbados at 13°N — panels face south at ~15° tilt. Lower elevation maximises sun exposure.
Water Treatment: 100-150m from residential (WHO). Near gully for treated water discharge. Downslope for gravity flow.
Premium (Hilltop): Highest elevation = best west coast views + maximum trade wind cooling. Lowest density (3-4/ac).
Entry (Near Gate): Highest density (7-8/ac) closest to community hub. First impressions + model home location.
