## 1. Site Context

### 1.1 Location
Mount Brevitor, St Peter Parish, Barbados — elevated hillside c. 250–400ft above sea level. Approximately 3km east of Speightstown. Views across the west coast of Barbados to the Caribbean Sea.

### 1.2 Trade Wind Orientation
Barbados sits in the northeast trade wind belt (NE→SW). This governs:
- **Farm + X Range placement**: Southeast — prevailing winds carry odour/noise *away* from residential
- **Premium cluster placement**: North, highest elevation — maximum cooling, best views
- **Entry cluster placement**: South, lower elevation — less wind exposure, appropriate for densest product

### 1.3 Adjacent Comparable — Vuemont Country Estate
39 acres, 35 units, immediately adjacent on Mount Brevitor. Primary pricing anchor:
- 2-bed apartment → USD $295K (BBD $520/sf)
- 2-bed villa → USD $350K (BBD $624/sf)
- 3-bed villa → USD $355K (BBD $456-582/sf)
- 5-bed plantation (Mt Brevitor) → USD $2.5M (BBD $5M)

## 2. Masterplan Layout — Hub-and-Spoke v3

### 2.1 Concept
**Hub-and-spoke layout**: Central Hub at the geometric centre. All 6 residential clusters within 10 minutes' walk. X Range + farm as a destination district (SE, 8–10 min walk). 4-level road hierarchy. Per-cluster pool + amenity building at each cluster.

### 2.2 Zone Placement

```
                    N ↑

         [CLUSTER E — Premium, hilltop, west views]
              [CLUSTER D — Upper, mid-north]
    [CLUSTER F — NW, YOTEL-ready, freed zone]
```

## 4. Per-Cluster Amenity Programme

### Design Principle
Each cluster has its own dedicated pool + amenity pavilion within 100m of every home. This is the defining feature benchmarked from The Lakes Dubai (pool per ~300 homes) and Arabian Ranches (pool per 150–300 homes). No equivalent exists in Barbados — MBE will be the first development to offer this model.

## 6. Farm + X Range District

- **Location**: Southeast, adjacent to gully — downwind from all residential (NE→SW trades)
- **Noise buffer**: 200m+ from nearest cluster (B), gully provides natural attenuation
- **Farm (17 acres)**: Aquaponics (tilapia + vegetables) + dairy + artisan cheese + farm shop
- **X Range (6 acres, on agri parcel)**: 25-bay covered driving range + bar/kitchen (40 covers)
- **Revenue**: Farm BBD 2.5M stabilised; X Range BBD 2.8M stabilised (operator TBC — risk O-02)
- **Access**: Dedicated service gate (SE) — farm/golf vehicles never on residential roads
