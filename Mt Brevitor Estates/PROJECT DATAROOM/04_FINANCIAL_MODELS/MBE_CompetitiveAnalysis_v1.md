**Residential Estate Development | St Peter, Barbados | 435 Units | BBD ~284M GDV**
*Prepared: 27 March 2026 | Coruscant Developments Inc*

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Mt Brevitor Estates is benchmarked against the most successful gated residential communities in the Gulf (Emaar, Wasl, Aldar) and the Caribbean (Sandy Lane, Royal Westmoreland, Apes Hill) to validate planning ratios, amenity provision, density, pricing, and investment positioning.

**Key finding:** MBE's per-cluster pool model (6 pools for 435 units = 1 pool per 73 homes) exceeds the UAE benchmark (1 per 150–300 homes) and is **unprecedented in Barbados** — where the existing standard is a single shared club pool for 250–400 units. This positions MBE as the Caribbean's first Dubai-standard lifestyle community at Caribbean pricing.

## 10. Investment Thesis

Mt Brevitor Estates is positioned to be **the most amenity-rich residential development in Barbados** at a price point accessible to local upgraders, diaspora buyers, and international remote workers.

**Bull case:** Full absorption at base-case pricing (BBD ~284M GDV), X Range + farm generating BBD 5.3M/yr stabilised income, YOTEL brand deal closes adding serviced apartment premium on Cluster F. Unlevered IRR: 22%+.

**Base case:** 90% of units sell at projected pricing, X Range operates with reduced revenue (BBD 2M/yr), farm at plan. Net margin: 16–18%. Unlevered IRR: 16–18%.

**Bear case:** 80% sell, pricing reduced 10%, X Range delayed 12 months, farm at 60% revenue. Net margin: 10–12%. Unlevered IRR: 12–14%. Development still profitable due to low land cost basis (BBD 9.25M for 120 acres = BBD 77K/acre).

**Key insight:** MBE's land cost (BBD 77K/acre) is roughly 1/10th of comparable Barbados west-coast land, creating a structural cost advantage that makes the development profitable even in the bear case.
